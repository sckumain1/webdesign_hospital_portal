Patient Rights and Responsibilities

Patient Rights:

1: To receive quality health care in a safe environment without discrimination because of race, colour, religion, nationality, disability, sex or age.
2:To be fully informed, before or during admission, of services available at this hospital.
3:To be informed if he/she requires care beyond the scope of this hospital.
4:To be fully informed on the expected approximate cost of the treatment at the time of admission.
5:To be treated with dignity, respect and courtesy in a non-judgmental and non-threatening manner and with consideration to the special preferences, psychosocial, spiritual and cultural needs that influence the patient’s perceptions of illness.
6:To be respected for personal dignity and privacy during examination, procedures and treatment, and to be free from all forms of abuse, neglect and harassment.
7:To have an access to his/her clinical records.
8:To know about the change in patient’s clinical condition.
9:To be informed of financial implication with the change of the category of services.
10:To be free from unnecessary use of physical or chemical restraint and/or seclusion as a means of coercion, convenience or retaliation. Restraints and/or seclusion will be used when clinically necessary.

Patient Responsibilities:

1:To provide correct and complete demographic information including full name, age, address and telephone number.
2:To provide accurate, correct and complete details of present and past illnesses, hospitalisations, medications, family history where relevant, all records of previous investigations and treatment and of allergic reactions especially sensitivity to any drug.
3:To follow the treatment plan recommended by those responsible for their care.
4:To cooperate with the staff of the hospital in receiving prescribed treatment.
5:To treat the hospital staff, other patients and visitors with courtesy, respect and dignity.
6:For their actions if they refuse treatment or do not follow the health care team’s instructions.
7:To pay hospital bills as promptly as possible and follow hospital rules and regulations. (To meet financial and other commitments promptly as agreed upon at the time of registration or admission.)
8:Follow Hospital Rules:Rules
9: To keep to the appointment schedule and to inform the hospital 24 hours in advance of any change.
10:To avoid speaking or behaving in a manner that disturbs other patient(s).
